Discord

The tournament will be held in the Gaming House Discord channel. Click here to join the channel!

Tournament Style: Double Elimination

  • All teams will play at the same time. Number of concurrent matches will depend on number of teams.
  • First loss will move you down to the losers bracket (still a chance to move on to finals after their first loss).
  • Two losses will eliminate a team.

Team Formation

Teams will be formed based on rank, KDA, most played agents, etc. based on their player stats on their tracker.gg.
