Rewards

Finalists (5)

Receives $10 worth of either VP (Valorant Points) or RP (Riot Points)

Individual (5)

Individual rewards will be given to players depending on specific categories. There are five individual rewards that are $10 each. Finalist Winners will be excluded from this category. If a finalist winner qualifies for an individual award, the second placed individual will receive the reward instead. Some individual rewards will be based on individual performance or voted based on charisma. Forms will be sent out within 24 hours after the tournament ends via Discord announcement channel.

Categories:

  • Highest average ACS
  • Best Clip (based on a player submission (at most 1 submission per player) and votes)
  • Two Most Positive Players (Based on votes)
  • Most Assist
